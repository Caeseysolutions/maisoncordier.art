Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Maison Cordier / CaeseySolutions LLC

Who we are

Maison Cordier operates under CaeseySolutions LLC, a Wyoming-registered company. We curate and introduce original artworks across Europe. This website is maisoncordier.art.

For all data-related enquiries: bonjour@maisoncordier.art

What data we collect

We collect personal data only when you choose to contact us. This includes:

  • Your name and email address (when you write to us by email)
  • Your phone number and message content (when you contact us via WhatsApp)
  • Any additional information you choose to share in your enquiry

We do not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising. We do not run advertising. We do not use third-party analytics tools that collect visitor data.

Why we collect it

Your contact information is collected solely to respond to your enquiry. We use it to:

  • Reply to questions about artworks, the artist, or the collection
  • Facilitate the sale and delivery of an artwork if you wish to purchase
  • Follow up on a conversation you have initiated

We will not use your data to send you unsolicited communications, newsletters, or marketing messages. If you wish to hear from us about future works, you must explicitly request this.

Legal basis (GDPR)

For visitors in the European Union and EEA, we process your personal data on the following lawful bases:

  • Legitimate interests — responding to enquiries you initiate
  • Contract performance — fulfilling a sale and delivery if agreed
  • Consent — where you have explicitly agreed to further contact

How we store and protect your data

Enquiry communications are handled via standard email and WhatsApp. We do not store your data on any additional databases or CRM systems. Your email correspondence is retained only as long as necessary to serve your enquiry or fulfil any transaction.

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. We do not transfer your data to third parties except where necessary to complete a delivery (e.g., providing your name and address to a shipping courier).

Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at bonjour@maisoncordier.art. We will respond within 30 days.

Third-party links

This site includes a link to kirsteenart.com, the artist's website. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of that site. Please review their privacy policy directly.

Children

This website is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The current version will always be available at this address. Material changes will be noted with an updated date at the top of this page.
